What is online

Oneline is a cloud based call forwarding system that can connect all your devices to a number and let you know about your missed calls from all these devices. After joining Oneline, the incoming call will be forwarded to the nearest phone that you added on your system.You will not have an unobserved missed call after joining Oneline.

Features

  • Call Everywhere

    For loved ones, your secretary will try to reach you by calling you everywhere at the same time.

  • Fast Notifications

    Oneline will contact you about missed calls periodically.

  • Blacklist

    Let Oneline deal with people you don't want to speak

  • Special Numbers

    You can choose a number with your name or business name.

  • Smart Group

    The right questions will be directed at the right people. No more losing time and missing a call.
